Lasagna

Description

A classic lasagna recipe that is well worth the time it takes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • dash garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 14.5 oz can tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 Tbsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brown meat slowly and then drain the grease.
  2. Add garlic powder, parsley, basil, salt, tomatoes and tomato paste.
  3. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes
  4. Boil noodles as directed on the box.
  5. Mix the cottage cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, dried parsley and parmesan cheese.
  6. Place 4 noodles in the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
  7. Spread half of the cheese mixture on top.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Add one third of the meat sauce over the cheese.
  10. Repeat the layering and then add a layer of noodles and then sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake at 375 for 30 minutes.
  13. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving.
