Calzones and Pizza Sauce

Description

A kid-friendly recipe that is easy to make. It can be personalized by using your favorite pizza toppings. Dip the calzones in the pizza sauce. Please make this pizza sauce, it is much better than store bought sauce. Our family of teens and adults eats 3 to 5 calzones each.

Ingredients

  • Rhodes roll dough
  • pizza toppings of your choice
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • dash onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Thaw the roll dough and flatten each roll into a circle.
  2. Fill each roll with about 1/4 cup of toppings.
  3. Fold one half of the dough over and seal the edges with a fork.
  4. Place each calzone on a greased cookie sheet and bake at 350 for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. For the sauce, mix all ingredients in a pot and cook over medium heat.
  6. Once bubbly, turn the heat down and simmer, covered, for 30 to 60 minutes until desired thickness.
