Broccoli Ham and Cheese Soup

Description

A family favorite hearty soup filled with chunks of potatoes, broccoli and ham. Great served in a bread bowl or with sliced bread to dip.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups broccoli, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • dash pepper

Steps

  1. Boil broccoli and potatoes until tender.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot and add flour. Cook for one minute.
  3. Add broth and milk and stir with a whisk until smooth.
  4. Add ham, cheese, salt, pepper, potatoes and broccoli.
